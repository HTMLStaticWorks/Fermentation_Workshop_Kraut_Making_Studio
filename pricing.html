Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership at any time? +

Absolutely. You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time with a single click from your profile settings page. No cancellation fees, no contracts, and no questions asked.

What is included in the 14-day Pro trial? +

The 14-day free trial gives you complete access to all 200+ lessons, recipes, premium videos, and certifications in our Pro plan. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged a single penny.

Are the live workshops recorded? +

Yes! Every single live session is recorded and made available in our master archive so you can rewatch or study them at your own convenience, even if you couldn't make it to the live stream.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing or groups? +

Yes, we offer custom specialized annual membership rates with up to 25% savings, as well as team and kitchen packages for schools, restaurants, or community organizations. Please contact our support team to learn more.
