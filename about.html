Our Story

15+ Years Fermenting
Our Foundation

From Grandma's Crock
to a Global Studio

It started in a small kitchen in Vermont, where our founder Marcus Keller watched his grandmother transform humble cabbage into something magical — alive, tangy, healing. That quiet ritual planted a seed.

After 15 years of hands-on fermenting, recipe development, and teaching local classes, Marcus launched KrautCraft in 2018 with one goal: make fermentation accessible to every kitchen on earth.

Today, KrautCraft has grown into the world's most trusted fermentation learning platform — with live workshops, online courses, an artisan shop, and a thriving community of 50,000+ fermenters across 70+ countries.

Our Journey

The KrautCraft Timeline

2009

The Crock That Started It All

Marcus makes his first sauerkraut in a clay crock gifted by his grandmother. He gives jars to neighbors — they keep asking for more.

2014

First Local Workshop

KrautCraft begins as a local community class in Vermont. 12 students, one crock, and a whiteboard full of microbiology.

2018

Online Platform Launches

KrautCraft.com goes live with 8 video courses. Within the first month, 2,000 students from 25 countries have enrolled.

2021

Artisan Shop Opens

The KrautCraft shop launches with hand-curated fermentation supplies, cultures, and equipment shipped worldwide.

2024

50,000 Fermenters Strong

KrautCraft crosses 50,000 active students across 70+ countries, with live workshops running every week of the year.
