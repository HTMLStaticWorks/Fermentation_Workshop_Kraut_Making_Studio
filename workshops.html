Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to join?

Absolutely not! Our Beginner's Path is designed for complete newcomers. All you need is a kitchen, some vegetables, and salt.

Are the courses live or on-demand?

We offer both. Most courses have on-demand video lessons you can watch anytime, plus live workshops that run weekly with real-time instructor interaction.

What equipment do I need?

For most beginner courses, just a mason jar, a kitchen knife, and salt. Advanced courses may need crocks or airlocks — our shop carries everything you need.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes! Completing any full course pathway awards you a KrautCraft Certificate of Completion. Pro and Studio members get industry-recognized certifications.

Can I access courses on mobile?

Yes. KrautCraft is fully mobile-responsive. Watch videos, follow recipes, and join live sessions from any device.
